Ingress Excubitor

Congratulations! You've found Excubitor, an app that works with Ingress.

What is Excubitor?

Excubitor is a helper application for Ingress, to manage your "entities destroyed" emails. Here's how to get started:

  1. Register your Google account on the site, so it can associate the incoming emails with you.
  2. Download and install the Android client, and set up your account.
  3. Set up your GMail (or other email) account to automatically forward all "entities destroyed" emails to the service.
  4. Wait for someone to destroy one or more of your resonators/links/fields.

... except you can't get started yet, because the project has only just begun! ☺

Why the name?

Excubitor is Latin for "sentinel" or "watchman". It's pronounced ex-OOH-bit-or.
